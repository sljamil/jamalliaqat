I'm Jamal Liaqat, an Electronics Engineer and Research Associate at Qosain Scientific, Lahore. I hold a BS in Avionics Engineering from the College of Aeronautical Engineering, NUST.

I thrive in research environments that demand precision: designing PCBs, building signal conditioning pipelines, writing firmware, and documenting it all clearly. I also bring web development skills with WordPress to help communicate science to broader audiences.
